WAVE

PauloCodifica's Version

Description

In WAVE you are a small cube that must avoid attacks from enemy cubes.

Move quickly, use your reflexes and dodge the enemies that move across the screen.

Resist the waves of enemies, defeat the evil boss cube and... Become the king of WAVE!


- The more waves you resist, the more score you get.

- You can also use your score in the store to buy upgrades and skins for your cube.

- Once you complete the game you will get a special skin.


Game originally created by RealTutsGML

Visit his YouTube channel: https://www.youtube.com/@RealTutsGML

If you are interested in learning how this game was developed or would like to program a similar one,
i recommend you to check out his playlist "Let's Build a Game in Java"

System Requirements

Operative System: Windows XP, Vista, 7, 8, 8.1, 10 / Mac OS / Linux

Processor: Intel Pentium 4 or better

Memory: 256mb RAM

Graphics: Any

Storage: 416 KB

Additional: You need to have JAVA installed
